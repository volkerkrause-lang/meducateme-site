About

Medical education with structure, clarity and clinical relevance.

MeducateMe aims to make paediatric learning more understandable by starting simple, then building up to the clinical detail needed for safe and confident practice.

The principle

Many learners are told what to do, but not why it works. MeducateMe focuses on the reasoning behind symptoms, signs, investigations and management decisions.

Topics

Core teaching areas

The first resources will focus on practical paediatric care, emergency assessment and revision-friendly explanations.

Paediatric emergencies

Recognition of the sick child, structured assessment, escalation and practical ward-based decision making.

EPALS-style revision

Scenario-based learning for resuscitation principles, deterioration, shock, airway and team communication.

Clinical reasoning

The physiology behind clinical findings, so learners can understand patterns rather than memorise lists.
